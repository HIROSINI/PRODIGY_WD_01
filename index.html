About Us

Welcome to our cozy cafe where you can enjoy delicious coffee and pastries. Our cafe is designed to provide a warm and inviting atmosphere for friends and family to gather and relax. Whether you're looking for a quick caffeine fix or a leisurely afternoon tea, we have something for everyone.

At our cafe, we pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients to craft our beverages and treats. Our baristas are highly skilled and passionate about their craft, ensuring that each cup of coffee is brewed to perfection and each pastry is baked with care.

Our Menu

Coffee

From rich and bold espressos to creamy lattes, our coffee selection is sure to please any coffee lover. Choose from:

  • Espresso - A strong and concentrated coffee beverage.
  • Cappuccino - Espresso topped with steamed milk and foam.
  • Latte - Espresso combined with steamed milk.
  • Americano - Espresso diluted with hot water.
  • Macchiato - Espresso "stained" with a small amount of steamed milk.

Tea

Experience the soothing flavors of our premium teas, ranging from classic black teas to exotic herbal blends. Our tea selection includes:

  • English Breakfast - A traditional black tea blend.
  • Earl Grey - Black tea flavored with bergamot oil.
  • Green Tea - Rich in antioxidants and known for its health benefits.
  • Bubble Tea - A Taiwanese tea-based drink with chewy tapioca pearls.
  • Chai - A spiced tea blend typically brewed with milk.
  • Peppermint - Refreshing and caffeine-free herbal tea.

Baked Items

Indulge in our freshly baked delights, perfect for any time of day. Choose from:

  • Croissants - Buttery and flaky pastries, available plain or filled.
  • Cookies - Deliciously sweet treats in various flavors.
  • Muffins - Moist and flavorful, available in assorted flavors.
  • Brownies - Rich and chocolatey, perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth.
